Diet Guidance

  • Start your day with a balanced breakfast including proteins and whole grains.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Incorporate fresh fruits and vegetables into every meal.
  • Avoid processed foods and opt for natural, whole foods whenever possible.
  • Plan portion sizes to avoid overeating.
  • Include healthy snacks like nuts, seeds, or yogurt between meals.
  • Maintain a consistent eating schedule for better digestion and energy levels.
